Has Ayurveda ignited your curiosity and interest in the mysterious dynamics of life unexplained by modern science yet? For over 25 years, since 1997, we have been fortunate to have facilitated and mentored the journey of thousands of students from all over the world in this ancient wisdom with a holistic yet modern approach. Based out of the home of Ayurveda, Kannur, Kerala, India, our school\u2019s mission is to create Ayurvedic practitioners of high credibility and knowledge through our variety of intense training courses in Ayurveda, Panchakarma, and Yoga. Currently, we offer 14 in-depth courses including Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Yoga, Traditional massages, Panchakarma, and Acupuncture that empower our students to practice them professionally. Our training courses in Ayurveda are accredited by the STED, (Scientific and Technical Education Development Council), Kerala, giving them international recognition.<\/p>\n
